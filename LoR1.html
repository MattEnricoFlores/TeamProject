The Darkin Saga: World Ender

Read for details on the fifth expansion of the Worldwalker set and the final expansion of The Darkin Saga.

Author: Matt Flores

World Ender is the fifth and final expansion of the Darkin Sage Set. It features 43 new cards with a focus on Targon with their new Champion Kayle. There have also been 2 Runeterra Champions: Aatrox and Ryze. This set also introduces the "Assimilate" and "Empowered" keywords to the game.

"Assimilate" is one of the two new keywords added and it is only on the card that Aatrox generates: World Ender. The text for the keyword reads: "If there are any allied Equipped with Darkin Keyword Equipment.png Equipment, transform them into Darkin units. If they are Champions, Level Up them after that". This effect is not only potentially game winning on its own, playing the card itself is also how Aatrox levels up which would usually bring a match to an end.

Unlike "Assimilate", "Empowered" is a new keyword that is shared with many cards that came with this expansion, notably the new Champion Kayle and all her follower units and some cards from other regions. The text reads "WHILE an Empower unit has its LoR Power.png Power at or above the listed number, they become Empowered and gain a bonus effect." While getting your units to be Empowered may look challenging, the expansion released many cards for Targon that allows them to easily increase the power of units both on the board and even in hand.

With that out of the way, here all all the new cards released for this expansion starting off with the Champion cards.
